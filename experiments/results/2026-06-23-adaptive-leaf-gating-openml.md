## Analysis (verdict: KEEP_EXPERIMENTAL)

This suite reports point means over 5 seeds (no per-seed std), so it supports
rank/direction statements only — **not** ≥1σ significance. Among RepLeaf arms, `adaptive`
has the best mean rank on both regression (4.50) and classification (3.80) and is never the
worst RepLeaf arm on any dataset; `adaptive_insample` is competitive on some datasets
(california, phoneme, vehicle) but worst of the four on adult — consistent with the LOO
leverage correction helping most on the binary noise-absorption case. With no dispersion
here, these are directional only and corroborate (do not independently establish) the
within-noise read from the real-data deep dive.

**Verdict: KEEP_EXPERIMENTAL** — no claim of general accuracy superiority; see
`2026-06-23-adaptive-leaf-gating-realdata.md` for the ≥1σ analysis. `adaptive` is per-leaf
model *selection* between constant and embedded-linear ridge leaves, shipped opt-in with the
default unchanged.
